Chapter 41: ABC

Place As, Bs and Cs so that

  • every row and column contains each letter exactly once
  • the closest letter to an edge cell is defined by that cell's letter.

Credit for this puzzle goes to Tetsuya Nishio & Naoki Inaba [22].

[22] http://inabapuzzle.com/honkaku/puzabc.html (in Japanese)

41.1 ABC controls

ABC shares much of its control system with Solo, Keen and Unequal (see section 30.1 for example).

An extra pencil mark is available in ABC. If you press 'X' it will put a cross inside a higlighted cell. This could be used for showing that a cell has to remain empty.

41.2 ABC parameters

Only a single parameter in this game. Square size is available from the ‘Custom...’ option on the ‘Type’ menu.
